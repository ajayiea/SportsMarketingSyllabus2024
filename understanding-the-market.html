Case Study: Understanding the Market

In this case study, students will explore consumer behavior and market segmentation strategies for the Olympic Games. The goal is to understand how different segments of the market respond to various marketing strategies and how to tailor marketing efforts to target these segments effectively.

Key Objectives:

  • Identify key consumer segments for the Olympic Games.
  • Analyze consumer behaviors and preferences.
  • Develop targeted marketing strategies for each segment.
