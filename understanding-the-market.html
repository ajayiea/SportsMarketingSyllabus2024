Overview

This case study explores how the organizers of the 2024 Olympics can better understand and engage with the diverse market segments of sports fans and participants. Students will examine consumer behavior and preferences to develop effective marketing strategies.

Objectives

  • Identify Key Consumer Segments: Determine the different groups within the audience for the 2024 Olympics.
  • Understand Motivations and Behaviors: Analyze what drives each segment's engagement with the Olympics.
  • Develop Targeted Marketing Strategies: Create strategies to effectively engage each consumer segment.

The Challenge

The 2024 Olympics will attract a broad audience, including avid sports fans, casual viewers, and participants from different cultural backgrounds. The challenge is to create marketing strategies that resonate with these diverse groups and maximize their engagement.

  1. Segment Identification: How to identify and define different consumer segments for the Olympics.
  2. Behavioral Insights: Understanding the needs and behaviors of each segment.
  3. Strategic Development: Crafting targeted marketing strategies for each identified segment.

Teaching Note

  • Consumer Behavior Research: Study consumer behavior patterns in the context of major sports events like the Olympics.
  • Market Segmentation: Discuss various methods for segmenting the market and targeting different groups.
  • Marketing Approaches: Explore how to develop and implement marketing strategies tailored to each segment.
