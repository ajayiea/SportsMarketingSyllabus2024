Overview

This case study explores the implementation and management of a marketing strategy for the 2024 Olympics. Students will develop a comprehensive plan to execute and oversee the marketing strategy, ensuring its effectiveness throughout the event.

Objectives

  • Develop a Plan: Create a detailed implementation plan for the marketing strategy of the 2024 Olympics.
  • Monitor and Evaluate: Learn techniques for assessing the effectiveness of marketing activities during the event.
  • Adjust Strategies: Understand how to adapt strategies based on real-time feedback and performance metrics.

The Challenge

Implementing and managing a marketing strategy for a global event like the 2024 Olympics involves addressing several key challenges:

  1. Execution: Ensuring the strategy is implemented smoothly and consistently across various platforms and regions.
  2. Monitoring: Continuously evaluating the effectiveness of marketing activities and campaigns.
  3. Adjustment: Making timely adjustments based on performance data and feedback to optimize results.
