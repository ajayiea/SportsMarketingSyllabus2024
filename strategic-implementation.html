Case Study: Strategic Implementation and Control

This case study addresses the execution and management of a sports marketing strategy during the Olympics. Students will examine how to monitor and control the effectiveness of their marketing strategies.

Key Objectives:

  • Implement a sports marketing strategy for the Olympics.
  • Monitor and assess the performance of marketing activities.
  • Adjust strategies based on performance metrics and feedback.
