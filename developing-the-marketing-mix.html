Case Study: Developing the Marketing Mix

This case study focuses on creating an effective marketing mix for a national Olympic campaign. Students will learn to balance the four Ps (Product, Price, Place, Promotion) to design a comprehensive marketing strategy.

Key Objectives:

  • Develop a product strategy for the Olympic Games.
  • Determine appropriate pricing strategies.
  • Plan distribution channels and promotional activities.
