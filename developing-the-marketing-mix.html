Overview

This case study focuses on crafting an effective marketing mix for the 2024 Olympics. Students will learn to design a strategy that includes product, price, place, and promotion to enhance the Olympic brand and appeal to a global audience.

Objectives

  • Develop a Product Strategy: Define the Olympic products and services to be offered and their unique attributes.
  • Create Pricing Strategies: Establish pricing that reflects the value and exclusivity of the Olympic experience.
  • Plan Distribution and Promotion: Develop effective strategies for distributing and promoting Olympic products and events.

The Challenge

To market the 2024 Olympics effectively, students need to balance the four Ps: Product, Price, Place, and Promotion. The challenge is to create a marketing mix that aligns with the Olympic brand and resonates with a diverse, international audience.

  1. Product: Identify and define the Olympic products and services that will attract global audiences.
  2. Price: Develop pricing strategies that capture the premium value of the Olympic experience while being accessible to different market segments.
  3. Place: Determine the most effective distribution channels for Olympic products and tickets.
  4. Promotion: Craft promotional strategies to build excitement and drive engagement with the Olympic brand.

Teaching Note

  • Product Strategy: Discuss how to create a compelling product strategy that highlights the unique aspects of the Olympics.
  • Pricing Strategy: Explore various pricing models and their impact on consumer perception and sales.
  • Distribution and Promotion: Review strategies for effectively distributing and promoting Olympic products and events.
